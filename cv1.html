About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a musician, photographer, and aspiring web developer with a passion for the arts.

Over the past 16 years, I have dedicated myself to guitar playing, creating emotionally rich sounds. Photography has become a vital part of my creative journey as well. Over three years, I've honed my skills in portrait photography and documenting everyday life and live events. I currently reside in the charming city of Prague, a constant source of inspiration for my artistic endeavors. Feel free to explore my portfolio where I showcase the results of my passion and creative approach to art. I'm excited to share this journey with you!
